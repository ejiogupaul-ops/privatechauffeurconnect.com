⚖️ Dispute Resolution Terms
You must read and agree to the following terms before raising a dispute.
1. Scope & Application
These Dispute Resolution Terms ("DRT") govern all post-trip vehicle condition disputes raised through the Private Chauffeur Connect platform. By raising a dispute you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms in full.
2. Evidence Requirement
You must supply photographic evidence of any alleged damage or discrepancy at the time of raising the dispute. Evidence submitted after the dispute window closes will not be accepted. Claims unsupported by photographic evidence will be dismissed.
3. Review Window
Disputes must be raised within the review window shown on the inspection screen. Once the window expires the chauffeur's payment is released automatically and disputes can no longer be initiated through the platform.
4. Escrow & Payment Hold
Upon submission of a valid dispute, the chauffeur's payment remains in escrow pending resolution. The platform does not release escrowed funds to either party during an active dispute without a resolution decision.
5. Investigation Process
The Private Chauffeur Connect disputes team will review all submitted evidence (pre-trip photos, post-trip photos, your uploaded evidence, and any written description) within 5 business days of the dispute being raised. Both parties may be contacted for additional information.
6. Resolution Outcomes
The disputes team may find in favour of the vehicle owner (compensation from escrow), in favour of the chauffeur (full escrow released to chauffeur), or split the award proportionally based on evidence. The platform's decision is final and binding.
7. False or Malicious Disputes
Raising a dispute on false, exaggerated, or fabricated grounds is a breach of the platform's Terms of Service. This may result in account suspension, liability for the chauffeur's lost earnings, and potential legal action.
8. Pre-Trip Baseline
Only damage or discrepancies that are visible in the post-trip photos but absent from the pre-trip inspection photos are eligible for compensation. Pre-existing conditions documented in pre-trip photos are excluded from any claim.
9. Claimed Amount
You may submit an estimated repair or replacement cost. This figure is used as guidance only and does not guarantee that amount will be awarded. Final compensation, if any, is determined by the disputes team based on all available evidence.
10. GPS & Metadata Verification
All inspection photos are geo-stamped and time-stamped at the point of capture. This metadata is used to verify authenticity. Tampering with metadata or submitting photos not taken during the trip will result in immediate dismissal of the dispute.
11. Chauffeur Notification
When a dispute is raised the chauffeur is notified immediately and given an opportunity to respond with their own written account and supplementary evidence. The disputes team will consider both accounts equally.
12. Escalation & External Resolution
If either party is dissatisfied with the platform's decision they may pursue resolution through the relevant small claims court or Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) scheme. The platform is not liable for costs incurred in external proceedings.
13. Data Retention
Dispute records, including all photos, written descriptions, and resolution decisions, are retained for 6 years in compliance with UK civil limitation periods and may be used as evidence in any legal proceedings.
14. Governing Law
These terms are governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales. Both parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales for the resolution of any disputes arising under these terms.
15. Amendments
Private Chauffeur Connect reserves the right to update these Dispute Resolution Terms at any time. Continued use of the platform following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. The version in effect at the time a dispute is raised shall govern that dispute.
