7.7 Outcomes

  • Shared liability (partial adjustment)
  • Chauffeur liable (deduction or insurance referral)

7.8 Automatic Closure

If no Dispute evidence is submitted within the applicable timeframe, the Booking will be automatically closed and Escrow funds released to the Chauffeur.
