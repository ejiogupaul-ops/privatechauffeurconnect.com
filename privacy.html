1. Introduction

Private Chauffeur Connect ("Private Chauffeur Connect", "we", "our", "us") operates the Private Chauffeur Connect mobile application and website ("Platform").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By creating an account or using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

For data protection purposes, Private Chauffeur Connect is the Data Controller.

Contact

privacy@privatechauffeurconnect.com

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

We may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Encrypted password credentials
  • Optional profile photograph

Vehicle Owner Information

We may collect:

  • Home address and postcode
  • Optional work address and postcode
  • Vehicle make, model, registration, and year

Chauffeur Information

To comply with legal obligations and maintain platform safety we collect:

  • Home address
  • Date of birth
  • Driving licence details
  • Private Hire / PCO licence details
  • National Insurance number
  • Identity verification documents
  • Bank account details for payouts
  • Professional documentation required for verification

Location Information

When a chauffeur is marked as Online, we collect real-time location data to:

  • Display availability
  • Match bookings
  • Navigate active journeys
  • Improve service reliability

Location tracking stops when the chauffeur goes offline or closes the application, except where required to complete an active booking.

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers and operating system
  • Browser type and app version
  • Crash logs, usage statistics, and security logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We process personal information for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing accounts
  • Matching vehicle owners with chauffeurs
  • Processing bookings and payments
  • Verifying chauffeur identity and licensing
  • Fraud prevention and platform security
  • Customer support
  • Improving Platform performance
  • Sending transactional communications
  • Meeting legal and regulatory obligations
  • Resolving disputes and enforcing our Terms of Service

We do not sell personal data.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR we rely on:

  • Performance of a contract
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests
  • User consent where required

Where consent is relied upon, it may be withdrawn at any time without affecting previous lawful processing.

5. Sharing Your Information

We only disclose information where necessary. This may include:

  • Vehicle owners and chauffeurs involved in the same booking
  • Payment providers
  • Identity verification providers
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Email delivery providers
  • Professional advisers, insurers, and legal representatives
  • Courts, regulatory bodies, and law enforcement agencies where legally required

All service providers are contractually required to protect personal information and may only process it on our instructions.

Disclosure for Penalty Notices & Legal Compliance

We may disclose limited chauffeur information to a vehicle owner where reasonably necessary to identify the driver of a vehicle during a booking, comply with legal obligations, respond to traffic offence notices, parking enforcement notices, insurance claims, or protect the legitimate interests of the parties involved.

Where a vehicle owner uploads a valid traffic penalty notice relating to a booking, Private Chauffeur Connect may contact the relevant chauffeur to confirm whether they were driving the vehicle at the relevant date and time. Upon confirmation by the chauffeur, and where necessary to enable the vehicle owner to comply with legal obligations or respond to the issuing authority, Private Chauffeur Connect may provide the vehicle owner with the chauffeur's relevant identifying information in accordance with applicable data protection laws. This processing is carried out on the basis of our legitimate interests and the legal obligation of the vehicle owner, and chauffeurs are informed of this possibility at the time of registration.
