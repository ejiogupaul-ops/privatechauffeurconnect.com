Professional Chauffeurs. Your Vehicle. Complete Peace of Mind.

Private Chauffeur Connect is a UK-based digital platform that connects Vehicle Owners with professional, vetted independent chauffeurs who can safely and professionally drive the owner's own vehicle.

Whether you're travelling to the airport, attending a business meeting, recovering from a medical procedure, or simply prefer not to drive, Private Chauffeur Connect makes it easy to book a qualified chauffeur who will drive your vehicle, not theirs.

We believe your vehicle should remain where it belongs: with you. Our platform provides a convenient alternative to hiring another vehicle while ensuring your journey is handled by experienced professionals.

What Makes Us Different

Private Chauffeur Connect is not a taxi company and we do not operate a fleet of vehicles.

Instead, we provide a technology platform that connects Vehicle Owners with independent professional chauffeurs.

Our platform manages the entire booking process, including:

  • Secure chauffeur bookings
  • Live journey tracking
  • Airport drop-off with vehicle return
  • Booking management
  • Secure payments
  • Booking records
  • Dispute resolution
  • Compliance management
  • Digital communication between Vehicle Owners and Chauffeurs
🛡️

Our Commitment to Safety

Safety and trust are at the heart of everything we do.

We are committed to ensuring that every chauffeur joining our platform meets our onboarding requirements before accepting bookings.

Our platform is designed with features that help protect everyone involved, including:

  • Chauffeur identity verification
  • Live GPS journey tracking
  • Digital booking records
  • Secure payment processing
  • Vehicle return confirmation
  • Key handover management
  • Incident reporting
  • Transparent dispute resolution

We continually review our processes and technology to improve the experience for both Vehicle Owners and Chauffeurs.
