Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Vehicle Owners

What is Private Chauffeur Connect?
▾

Private Chauffeur Connect is a platform that connects vehicle owners with vetted, independent professional chauffeurs. You use your own vehicle, while a professional chauffeur provides the driving service.

What services can I book?
▾

You can book chauffeurs for:

  • Airport transfers
  • Business travel
  • Special events
  • Weddings
  • Evening engagements
  • Medical appointments
  • Long-distance journeys
  • Vehicle return services
  • Hourly chauffeur hire
How do I book a chauffeur?
▾

Simply:

  1. Enter your journey details.
  2. Choose the service you require.
  3. Review the price.
  4. Confirm and pay securely.

A qualified chauffeur will be assigned to your booking.

Can I track my vehicle?
▾

Yes. During an active booking, you can view the chauffeur's live location through the app.

For Airport Drop-Off with Vehicle Return bookings, tracking continues while your vehicle is being returned until the booking is completed.

What if my flight is delayed or cancelled?
▾

Depending on your booking, airport waiting time can be extended (additional charges may apply). If your flight is cancelled, please contact your chauffeur or our support team through the app as soon as possible.

What happens if I need to cancel?
▾

You can cancel your booking through the app. Refunds are calculated based on:

  • Time before pickup
  • Booking status
  • Whether the chauffeur has started travelling

Any applicable cancellation charges will be shown before you confirm cancellation.

Is my payment secure?
▾

Yes. All payments are processed securely through our payment provider. Your payment is held and managed in accordance with our booking and cancellation policies.

What insurance is required?
▾

Private Chauffeur Connect is developing an insurance solution specifically for bookings made through the platform. Where applicable, insurance requirements will be communicated before your booking is confirmed.

How are chauffeurs vetted?
▾

Every chauffeur must complete our onboarding process, which may include:

  • Identity verification
  • Licence checks
  • Right-to-work verification
  • Background screening (where applicable)
  • Document verification
  • Agreement to our platform standards

All requirements must be satisfied before a chauffeur can accept bookings.

What happens if there's a problem during my booking?
▾

If you experience an issue, you can report it directly through the app. Our support team will investigate using booking records, GPS history and other available evidence before reaching a decision.

🚗

Chauffeurs

Who can join Private Chauffeur Connect?
▾

Professional independent chauffeurs who meet our onboarding requirements and agree to our platform standards.

Am I employed by Private Chauffeur Connect?
▾

No. Chauffeurs remain independent contractors and are responsible for managing their own business and tax affairs.

How do I receive bookings?
▾

Bookings are offered through the app based on availability and other operational factors. You can accept or decline available bookings.

How do payouts work?
▾

Payments are made to your chauffeur wallet after completed bookings. Funds are then transferred according to the platform's payout schedule. Where applicable, cancellation compensation is also credited to your wallet.

How much commission does the platform charge?
▾

Private Chauffeur Connect deducts its agreed platform commission before calculating chauffeur earnings. Your earnings are displayed before you accept each booking.

What happens if a Vehicle Owner cancels?
▾

Depending on the timing and booking status, you may receive cancellation compensation in accordance with our cancellation policy. If compensation is due, it will be added to your wallet for your next payout.

What if I need to cancel a booking?
▾

You should only cancel when absolutely necessary. Repeated cancellations may affect your platform rating and could result in temporary suspension or account review.

What happens if there's an accident?
▾

You must:

  1. Ensure everyone is safe.
  2. Contact emergency services where required.
  3. Notify the Vehicle Owner.
  4. Report the incident through the app immediately.
  5. Follow our incident reporting procedure.
What if I can't return the keys as instructed?
▾

If you cannot safely follow the Vehicle Owner's Key Return Instructions, contact the Vehicle Owner through the app before taking any alternative action. Never leave keys unattended unless specifically authorised.

Can I take another passenger while returning the owner's vehicle?
▾

No. Only authorised occupants connected with the booking may be carried. Unauthorised passengers are strictly prohibited.

Am I tracked during bookings?
▾

Yes. GPS tracking remains active throughout active bookings to support journey monitoring, booking records and dispute resolution.

What support is available?
▾

Our support team is available to assist with booking issues, payment queries, incidents and disputes through the app.
